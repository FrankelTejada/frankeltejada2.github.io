About Me

Hi, my name is Frankel; I am passionate about data analytics and how data can be used to make better decisions. I hold a Bachelor’s Degree in Business Administration and Management (Kutztown University of Pennsylvania) and Google Data Analytics Certificate (Coursera/Google). At an early age, I have always found myself infactuated by puzzles, the more complex the better. So that’s how I look at big data sets: to me it is one big puzzle I want to solve. Finding patterns and stories told through data is fulfilling to me. I strongly believe in open data and open source software. I am skilled in SQL, Tableau, Spreadsheets, and also have experience with R.

I have great deal of experience in the Business Management field, as well as being in a leadership position. I enjoy working with fellow team members and seeing them grow as individuals. When I'm not cleaning, processing, and/or analyzing data, I enjoy, basketball, Brazilian Jiu Jitsu and spending time with my wife and 2 kids.
